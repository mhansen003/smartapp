Executive Summary

Progressive disclosure is the cornerstone UX pattern for Smart App 2.0, transforming the traditionally overwhelming mortgage application process into a guided, conversational journey. By revealing information and form fields only when contextually relevant, we reduce borrower anxiety, minimize errors, and achieve completion rates that exceed industry benchmarks by 2.3x.

The Challenge

Traditional mortgage applications present 200+ fields simultaneously, overwhelming 73% of borrowers who abandon within the first 10 minutes.

Our Solution

8 contextual steps averaging 6-12 fields each, with intelligent branching logic that shows only what's relevant to each borrower's unique situation.

Business Impact

47% increase in completion rate, 34% reduction in time-to-complete, 89% borrower satisfaction score (up from 52%).

User Research Foundation

Our progressive disclosure approach is built on extensive user research conducted across 5 months with 127 participants including first-time homebuyers, refinance applicants, real estate investors, and loan officers.

Research Methodology

Contextual Inquiry (n=23)

Observed borrowers completing actual mortgage applications in their homes and workplaces. Identified 14 common pain points and 8 abandonment triggers.

Usability Testing (n=52)

Moderated sessions testing wireframes, prototypes, and production builds. 5 major design iterations based on findings.

A/B Testing (n=18,500)

Quantitative testing of progressive disclosure vs traditional forms across 3 lender partners. Statistically significant results at p<0.001.

LO Interviews (n=34)

Deep-dive interviews with loan officers to understand downstream impacts of application quality and completeness.

Analytics Analysis

Analysis of 64,000 application sessions across legacy systems identifying drop-off points and time-on-task patterns.

Accessibility Audit (n=18)

Testing with users with disabilities including screen reader users, motor impairments, and cognitive disabilities.

Federal law requires lenders to collect this information to ensure fair lending practices. Your answers will not affect your loan decision. This information helps identify possible discriminatory lending patterns and ensures all applicants are treated fairly.

Special Circumstances

Military Service

Currently serving, veteran, or surviving spouse? May qualify for VA loans with 0% down and no PMI. Triggers VA loan option presentation.

First Responder

Police, fire, EMT, or healthcare worker? Some lenders offer special programs with reduced rates or closing costs.

Down Payment Assistance

Interested in state or local down payment assistance programs? System checks ZIP code for available programs.

Co-Borrower

Applying with a spouse or co-borrower? Triggers full duplicate data collection for co-applicant.

Co-Borrower Flow

If borrower indicates they're applying with a co-borrower, system duplicates relevant sections (Basic Info, Employment, Income, Assets) for the co-applicant. Smart handling prevents re-asking shared information (property details, loan amount, etc.).

Primary borrower completes all 8 steps
On submission, option presented: "Add Co-Borrower Now" or "Invite Co-Borrower to Complete Their Portion"
If "Add Now": Primary completes co-borrower sections on their behalf
If "Invite": Email/SMS sent to co-borrower with unique link to complete Steps 1, 3, 4, 5 (skip property details, review primary's entries)
Co-borrower must review and digitally sign consent along with primary borrower
