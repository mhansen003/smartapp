Strategic Initiatives (6-12 Months)

Long-term vision and competitive differentiators for market leadership.

STRATEGIC: 6-12 Month Vision

1. Predictive Analytics & ML Models
Use historical data to predict application completion, approval likelihood, identify at-risk applications
2. Multi-Language Support (Spanish)
Full Spanish translation for borrower experience, capturing 18% market expansion
3. Native Mobile Apps (iOS/Android)
Native apps for enhanced mobile experience beyond responsive web
4. White-Label Multi-Tenant Architecture
Enable multiple lenders to use platform with custom branding
5. Advanced AI Features
Voice-based data entry, intelligent field suggestions, predictive prefill
6. Video Verification Integration
Live video ID verification as Prove fallback or premium option
7. Marketplace Integrations
Third-party CRMs (Salesforce, HubSpot), marketing automation, referral networks
8. Borrower Referral Program Automation
Incentivize completed borrowers to refer friends with automated tracking

Risk Mitigation Priorities

Technical Risks

Risk Likelihood Impact Mitigation Strategy
Vendor API downtime/instability Medium High Circuit breakers, graceful degradation, fallback flows
Data quality issues from vendors High Medium Confidence scoring, user verification, LO review flags
Performance degradation at scale Low High Load testing, caching strategy, auto-scaling
Security breach or data leak Low Critical Defense-in-depth, encryption, pen testing, monitoring

Business Risks

Risk Likelihood Impact Mitigation Strategy
LO adoption resistance Medium High Configuration dashboard, change management, training
Borrower abandonment rates Medium High Progressive disclosure, mobile-first, save/resume
Competitive market entry High Medium Fast iteration, unique features (LO config, AI assistant)
Compliance/regulatory changes Medium Medium Flexible architecture, compliance monitoring, legal review
