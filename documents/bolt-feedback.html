Yaz's Key Feedback Points

Integration of Title Data

For refinance scenarios, the application should incorporate title data integration to automatically identify individuals on the title and any existing liens, simplifying the application process.

Clear Mapping of Vendor Interactions

Define how and when user data is pulled from vendors (proof of identity, employment verification) and ensure efficient handling of integrations without overwhelming the user.

Gamification of Tasks

Structure tasks so users can complete them in manageable segments with sequential workflows, receiving notifications about what to do next. Motivational prompts or rewards for completing tasks promptly.

Visual Timeline Concerns

Current visual timeline takes up too much screen space. Minimize timeline space to allow tasks to be more prominent, enabling users to dive straight into tasks without distractions.

AI Integration for User Assistance

Develop AI assistant that proactively engages with users based on their actions. If a user hesitates on a question, AI could offer contextual help or educational information.

Management of Consent and Credit Checks

Create user-friendly interface allowing borrowers to easily understand and manage their consent for credit checks and other data pulls. Clarity on soft vs hard credit checks.
