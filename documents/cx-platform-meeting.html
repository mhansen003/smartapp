Prototype Features Demonstrated

Prove Integration

Identity verification via Prove - "magic link" sent via text to verify borrower and pre-fill data (name, email, phone, SSN). Strengthens data security and reduces manual entry.

Co-borrower Handling

Improved flow for adding co-borrowers with options to invite them or complete jointly. System can leverage Prove for co-borrower verification as well.

AI Assistant Integration

Universal AI assistant available throughout flow. Can trigger based on time spent on page. Replaces static tooltips with dynamic, contextual help.

Smart Data Collection

Intentional question selection - only ask what's needed. Use pre-filled data to avoid duplicate questions (e.g., don't ask work address if we'll get it from VOI providers).

Income Calculator

Built-in calculator for borrowers who get paid hourly to help them estimate annual income. Addresses common pain point identified in LO interviews.

Task-Based Completion

Shorter initial application followed by gamified task workflow. First step already completed upon submission, encouraging users to continue with remaining tasks.

Co-Branding Throughout

Consistent co-branding with LO and realtor partner information visible throughout the application, maintaining trust and connection.

Calendly Integration

Borrowers can immediately schedule a call with their LO using integrated Calendly link. Easy feature with high value for user engagement.

Paul's Strategic Vision Discussion

The Automation Philosophy

Paul outlined his vision for the "smart LO of the future" who doesn't want to collect docs manually - they want the system to collect docs for them. This led to the "AI Future vs Stone Age" framework.

Paul's Ideal Automated Flow:

1. Application submitted ‚Üí Soft credit automatically pulled
2. Soft credit matched with FlueID title data for property/liability matching
3. Pre-run AUS with prefilled sample data
4. Generate needs list from Candor integration based on AUS results
5. Populate task management system automatically
6. Deliver complete summary to LO with all enriched data

The LO Receives:

"Hey David, you got this guy Tim who just applied for a loan. Here's everything that I did. I believe Tim is going to be based on this thing. Here's a uniqueness based on the AI searches that he has. His score is an 820. He had questions in this space here. He's already uploaded Truv. You don't need to worry about that. Kudos. You don't got to chase it. We put it through the process. He already gave E consent and he's already signed his borrower's authorization. Additionally, he uploaded a picture of his driver's license, which we scanned with AI and confirmed it all matches up. We've already validated the liabilities because we did a match up with FlueID and just confirmed with him. He's got two additional investment properties that were originally missed in the application that we caught. And here's the information you need to know about this. The payments are including taxes and insurance. We validated with him. Based on your pricing, we just ran it for you and we ran it based on your LC behavior at 45 basis point LC on conventional 101. We think you're going to be able to make this payment and his DTI is probably going to be that. Let me know if there's anything I can help as your AI assistant."
