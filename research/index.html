Research Framework

Phase 1: Discovery

Exploratory research to understand current state, user needs, pain points, and opportunities through in-depth interviews and stakeholder sessions.

Phase 2: Synthesis

Analysis and synthesis of research findings to identify patterns, themes, and actionable insights. Creation of frameworks and recommendations.

Phase 3: Validation

Concept testing with prototypes and mockups to validate design decisions and gather feedback before development.

Phase 4: Implementation

Translating insights into actionable requirements, design specifications, and development roadmap.

Top 10 Research Insights

The most impactful insights that emerged from our comprehensive research initiative, representing the core themes that shaped our product strategy.

1

Borrowers Abandon Due to Complexity

The current application is overwhelming with too many fields presented at once. Users need progressive disclosure and guidance throughout the journey.

User Experience High Impact
2

LOs Need Visibility Into Borrower Progress

Loan Officers expressed frustration with not knowing where borrowers are stuck. Real-time progress tracking and intervention points are critical.

LO Tools High Impact
3

Instant Identity Verification Builds Trust

Phone-centric identity verification (Prove) was universally praised. Borrowers love the instant verification and data prefill experience.

Integration Trust & Security
4

Mobile Experience is Non-Negotiable

Over 60% of applications start on mobile devices. Mobile-first design and seamless cross-device experiences are critical for success.

Mobile High Impact
5

Document Upload Friction Causes Drop-Off

Manual document uploads are a major pain point. Integration with bank accounts (Plaid) and payroll systems (TRUV) significantly reduces friction.

User Experience Integration
6

Co-Borrower Collaboration is Critical

Most applications involve multiple borrowers, but current systems don't support collaboration well. Seamless co-borrower workflows are essential.

Collaboration Medium Impact
7

LOs Want Flexible Configuration Options

Different LOs have different workflows and preferences. Configurable features and toggles allow personalization without sacrificing consistency.

LO Tools Configuration
8

Clear Progress Indicators Improve Completion

Users need to know where they are and how much is left. Visual progress indicators and step completion status significantly improve completion rates.

User Experience Completion Rates
9

AI Assistant Reduces Support Burden

Context-aware AI assistance helps borrowers answer questions in real-time, reducing the need for LO intervention on simple queries.

AI/ML Support
10

Soft Credit Pulls Enable Early Qualification

Early soft credit pulls help borrowers understand their options without score impact, improving conversion and reducing disappointment later.

Credit Qualification
