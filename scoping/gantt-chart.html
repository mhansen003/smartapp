Executive Summary

The Smart App 2.0 project is a comprehensive digital transformation initiative spanning 14 months with a phased approach to delivery. The project began with Discovery & Research in November 2024, leading to MVP development from January-March 2025, followed by Beta Testing in April-May, and culminating in a June 15, 2025 launch date.

Post-launch, the project continues with iteration phases through August and scaling/enhancement efforts through December 2025. The critical path focuses on core platform development, third-party integrations (Prove, Plaid, TRUV), and LO dashboard capabilities. Peak team capacity of 18 members will be reached during Phase 2 (Beta Testing) with strategic resource allocation across development, design, QA, and product management functions.

Mitigation Strategies

  • Database Schema: Early architecture review with stakeholders. Freeze schema by Jan 31.
  • Authentication: Parallel development of OTP and Prove. Dedicated integration engineer assigned.
  • Third-Party APIs: Sandbox environments set up early. Weekly sync with vendor support teams.
  • Security Audit: Continuous security testing during development. Pre-audit in early May.
  • Beta Testing: Beta participants recruited in advance. Test scripts prepared early.
