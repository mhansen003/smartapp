Great question! Let me explain the difference:

Base Income is your regular salary or hourly wages before any bonuses, overtime, or commissions. For example, if you earn $5,000/month as your base salary, that's your base income.

Total Gross Income includes your base income PLUS any additional earnings like:

  • Overtime pay
  • Bonuses
  • Commissions
  • Tips (if applicable)
  • Rental income

So if your base is $5,000/month and you regularly earn $500 in overtime, your total gross income would be $5,500/month.

Example Conversations from POC

Example 1: Self-Employed Income Question

Borrower: I'm self-employed. How do I calculate my monthly income?
AI Assistant: For self-employed borrowers, we calculate your monthly income using your tax returns from the past 2 years. Here's how:

1. Take your net profit from Schedule C (or K-1 if you're a partner/shareholder)
2. Add back any depreciation, depletion, or one-time losses
3. Average the adjusted amounts from 2 years
4. Divide by 24 to get monthly income

Example: If your 2023 net profit was $72,000 and 2024 was $84,000 (total $156,000), your average annual income is $78,000, which equals $6,500/month.

üí° You'll need your complete tax returns (1040 + all schedules) for the past 2 years. Your loan officer may also request your most recent profit & loss statement.

Safety, Compliance & Limitations

What AI Will NOT Do

No Legal Advice: Will not advise on contracts, legal matters, or regulatory compliance questions
No Financial Advice: Will not recommend specific loan products or tell borrower what decision to make
No Rate Quotes: Will not provide interest rates or lock rates (defers to LO)
No Guarantee of Approval: Will not promise loan approval or make qualification determinations
No PII Collection: Will not ask for SSN, account numbers, passwords, or sensitive data via chat
No Modifications to Application: Cannot edit borrower data, only provide guidance on how to enter it

Compliance Safeguards

All conversations logged and reviewable by compliance team
Automatic detection of prohibited topics (discrimination, steering, red-lining)
Responses reviewed quarterly by compliance officer for accuracy
Clear disclaimer shown: "AI suggestions are informational. Your LO makes final decisions."
ECOA compliance: AI provides identical guidance regardless of borrower demographics
Escalation triggers for sensitive topics (bankruptcy, foreclosure, credit disputes)

Data Privacy & Security

Conversations encrypted in transit (TLS 1.3) and at rest (AES-256)
No training on borrower-specific data (maintains Claude's zero-retention policy)
PII automatically redacted from logs (SSN, account numbers masked)
SOC 2 Type II compliant infrastructure (Anthropic's API)
GDPR compliant: Borrowers can request conversation deletion
