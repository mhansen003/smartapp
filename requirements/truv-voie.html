Overview

TRUV (The Registry of Unverified income & Verification) connects directly to payroll systems like ADP, Paychex, Gusto, and 160+ other providers. Borrowers securely log in to their payroll account, and TRUV instantly retrieves employment history, income details, YTD earnings, tax withholdings, and more - replacing weeks of document chasing with a 2-minute process.

Automated Income Calculations

Monthly Income (Stable Employment)

For salaried employees with consistent pay:

Monthly Income = (YTD Gross / Months Employed) √ó 1.0

Example:
YTD Gross (Jan 1 - Sep 30) = $67,500
Months = 9
Monthly Income = $67,500 / 9 = $7,500

Variable Income (Bonus/Commission)

For employees with variable compensation:

Base Monthly = Regular Salary
Bonus/Comm = (Last 24mo Total / 24) √ó Continuity Factor

Continuity Factor:
- 2+ years same employer: 1.0
- 1-2 years: 0.75
- <1 year: 0.5

Hourly Employees

For hourly workers with variable hours:

Monthly = (Total Hours Last 12mo / 12) √ó Hourly Rate

Example:
Avg Hours/Month = 168
Hourly Rate = $22.50
Monthly Income = 168 √ó $22.50 = $3,780

Overtime Calculation

Overtime included if consistent for 2+ years:

IF (Overtime hours > 10% of total hours) AND
(2+ year history of OT) THEN
Include Average OT in monthly income

Else: Exclude overtime from calculations

LO Review & Override Capabilities

While TRUV automates verification, Loan Officers retain full control to review, adjust, and override automated calculations when necessary.

LO Action Capability Use Case
View Raw Data Access complete pay stub PDFs and data dump Verify unusual income patterns
Adjust Income Manually override calculated monthly income Exclude one-time bonuses or include expected raise
Exclude Pay Periods Remove specific pay stubs from calculation Remove anomalies (unpaid leave, one-time payments)
Add Notes Document rationale for adjustments Explain to underwriter why override was necessary
Request Re-verification Ask borrower to reconnect and pull fresh data Data is stale (>30 days old)
Supplement with Docs Request additional paystubs or W-2s manually TRUV data incomplete or needs validation

Compliance & Audit Trail

FCRA Compliance: TRUV is a Consumer Reporting Agency (CRA) compliant with Fair Credit Reporting Act
ECOA Compliance: All borrowers given equal access to automated verification regardless of employer size
Consent Management: Explicit borrower consent recorded with timestamp before data retrieval
Data Retention: Income verification data retained for 25 months post-loan for QC/audits
Audit Logging: Complete audit trail: who accessed data, when, what changes were made
Adverse Action: If TRUV data causes denial, automated adverse action notice generation
Dispute Resolution: Borrowers can dispute TRUV data; revalidation process defined
