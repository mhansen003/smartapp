Overview

The Soft Pull Credit integration allows Loan Officers to assess a borrower's creditworthiness early in the process without triggering a hard inquiry that impacts credit scores. This enables accurate pre-qualification, helps borrowers understand their options, and reduces wasted effort on applications unlikely to succeed. Unlike hard pulls, soft pulls can be performed multiple times without penalty.

Integration Purpose

Provide accurate credit assessment for pre-qualification without negatively impacting borrower credit scores.

Business Value

Reduce application fallout by 40%, improve loan product matching, and set accurate borrower expectations early.

Implementation Status

Integrated with Experian and Equifax. TransUnion integration in progress. Production ready for soft pull only.

Borrower Communication & Transparency

Consent Language Example

Credit Inquiry Authorization

By checking this box, I authorize [Lender Name] to obtain a soft credit inquiry from one or more consumer reporting agencies for the purpose of evaluating my eligibility for a mortgage pre-qualification. I understand that:

  • This is a "soft pull" that will NOT impact my credit score
  • This inquiry will only be visible to me on my credit report, not to other lenders
  • This is for pre-qualification purposes only; a hard credit pull will be required for final approval
  • My information will be handled securely in compliance with all applicable privacy laws

Error Scenarios & Recovery

Error Type Cause User Message Recovery Action
THIN_FILE Borrower has insufficient credit history "We couldn't find enough credit history to generate a score. You may still qualify through alternative verification." Escalate to LO, offer manual underwriting path
FROZEN_CREDIT Borrower has security freeze on credit file "Your credit file appears to be frozen. Please temporarily lift the freeze with {BUREAU} and try again." Provide bureau unfreeze instructions, retry button
SSN_MISMATCH SSN doesn't match name/DOB in bureau records "We couldn't verify your information. Please double-check your SSN and date of birth." Allow correction, offer LO assistance after 2 failures
BUREAU_TIMEOUT Credit bureau API not responding within 30 seconds "The credit bureau is experiencing delays. We'll retry automatically in a moment." Auto-retry with exponential backoff (3 attempts)
BUREAU_UNAVAILABLE Bureau API returning 503 (maintenance) "Credit services are temporarily unavailable. Please try again in 15 minutes, or your LO can run this manually." Queue for background retry, notify LO
FRAUD_ALERT Credit file has fraud alert or identity theft flag "There's a fraud alert on your credit file. Please contact your loan officer to proceed." Immediate LO notification, manual verification required
DECEASED_INDICATOR SSN flagged as deceased in credit bureau data "We encountered an issue with your credit file. Your loan officer will contact you shortly to resolve this." High-priority LO alert, manual resolution (likely SSN error)
