Overview

Plaid's Verification of Assets (VOA) product allows borrowers to instantly connect their bank accounts, providing real-time balance and transaction data. This eliminates the need for manual bank statement uploads and significantly accelerates the asset verification process during mortgage applications.

Integration Purpose

Enable borrowers to securely link bank accounts and automatically verify asset balances without uploading statements.

Business Value

Reduce verification time from days to minutes, improve data accuracy, and enhance borrower experience.

Implementation Status

Integrated in Smart App POC. Production deployment pending final security review and LO training.

Integration Workflow

Step Action User Experience System Process
1 Initiate Connection Borrower clicks "Connect Bank Account" button Launch Plaid Link modal
2 Select Institution Search and select their bank from 12,000+ institutions Plaid presents institution search interface
3 Authenticate Enter online banking credentials Plaid securely authenticates with bank
4 Select Accounts Choose which accounts to link (checking, savings, etc.) Display available accounts from bank
5 Consent Review and accept data sharing consent Record consent timestamp and scope
6 Verify Balances Review account balances and confirm Retrieve balance data via Plaid API
7 Store & Process See "Verified" status on assets section Store account data, calculate liquid assets

Security & Compliance

All data encrypted in transit (TLS 1.3) and at rest (AES-256)
No storage of borrower banking credentials
Compliance with GLBA, FCRA, and ECOA regulations
SOC 2 Type II certified infrastructure
Annual third-party security audits
PII data retention limited to regulatory requirements

Error Scenarios & Handling

Error Type Cause User Message Resolution
INSTITUTION_NOT_AVAILABLE Bank temporarily down for maintenance "Your bank is currently unavailable. Please try again in a few hours." Retry later or use manual upload
INVALID_CREDENTIALS Wrong username/password entered "The credentials you entered don't match. Please try again." Retry with correct credentials
MFA_NOT_SUPPORTED Bank requires MFA method Plaid doesn't support "We can't connect to this account automatically. Please upload statements." Fallback to manual statement upload
ITEM_LOGIN_REQUIRED Bank connection needs reauthorization "Please reconnect your bank account to refresh your information." Relaunch Plaid Link to reconnect
INSUFFICIENT_CREDENTIALS Bank requires additional security questions "Your bank needs additional verification. Please complete the security questions." Follow Plaid prompts for additional auth
