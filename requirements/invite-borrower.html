Overview

The LO-Initiated Borrower Invitation system empowers Loan Officers to proactively start applications for their borrowers. After an initial phone conversation or lead capture, LOs can send a branded, personalized invitation via email or SMS that directs the borrower to begin their Smart App journey. This includes support for co-borrower invitations, tracking of invitation status, and automated follow-up reminders to maximize conversion.

Primary Use Case

LO initiates application after lead qualification call, borrower clicks link to begin without manual account creation.

Business Value

Increase application start rate by 62%, reduce time-to-application from 3 days to hours, improve borrower experience.

Implementation Status

Fully implemented in POC. 78% email open rate, 54% click-through rate, 41% conversion to application start.

Co-Borrower Invitation Flow

Two Invitation Methods

Method 1: LO Invites Both Upfront

LO enters both borrower and co-borrower details when creating invitation
System sends separate invitations to each email address
Applications are linked in database (shared application ID)
Each borrower completes their own sections independently
Both can see overall progress, but only edit their own data
LO sees combined view of both borrowers' completion status

Method 2: Primary Invites Co-Borrower

LO invites primary borrower only
During application, primary sees "Add Co-Borrower" option
Primary enters co-borrower email and phone
System auto-sends invitation to co-borrower with context: "[Primary Name] has invited you..."
Co-borrower accepts invitation, creates their own session
Applications merge into joint application

Co-Borrower Coordination Rules

Scenario System Behavior Notifications
Primary completes their sections before co-borrower starts Co-borrower sees "Your co-borrower has completed their part. Here's what you need to do..." Email to co-borrower: "Don't keep [Primary] waiting!"
Co-borrower completes before primary Primary sees banner: "Your co-borrower has finished! Complete yours to submit." Email to primary: "[Co-Borrower] has completed their application"
Both working simultaneously Real-time sync of shared fields (property address, loan amount) No notifications, auto-save prevents conflicts
One borrower edits shared field Update visible to both, last-write-wins In-app notification: "[Name] updated the property address"
Application ready to submit Both must click "Review & Submit" before LO can access Email to both: "Your joint application is ready to submit"
