Overview

The LO Configuration System empowers Loan Officers to customize the Smart App experience for their borrowers. This includes controlling which features are enabled, setting default values, customizing branding elements, and determining workflow behaviors. Research shows that LOs have diverse preferences and this flexibility is critical for adoption.

Purpose

Enable LOs to tailor the borrower experience while maintaining compliance and data quality standards.

Research Insight

7 LO interviews revealed strong preferences for control over borrower communications and feature visibility.

Implementation

Toggle system with sensible defaults, overridable at LO level with admin oversight capabilities.

Research-Driven Insights

üí° Key Finding

"I need to be able to turn things on and off based on my borrower. Not all situations are the same."

- CJ Nelson, Loan Officer

üéØ Design Principle

Default settings should reflect the "happy path" for most scenarios, but LOs must have the ability to override based on their specific borrower relationships and business practices.

‚ö†Ô∏è Critical Requirement

Configuration changes must be tracked and auditable. Some settings may require compliance approval before activation, particularly those affecting data collection or disclosures.

Future Configuration Enhancements

Borrower-specific overrides (apply settings to individual borrowers)
Configuration templates (save and reuse sets of configurations)
Team-level defaults (apply settings across LO teams)
A/B testing capabilities (test configuration impact on completion rates)
Configuration analytics (show which settings correlate with better outcomes)
Seasonal toggles (automatically enable/disable based on market conditions)
