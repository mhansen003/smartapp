üë• TEAM OVERVIEW

The Smart App 2.0 team is a cross-functional organization of 24 professionals spanning product management, engineering, design, UX research, consulting, and field operations (loan officers). We work together to transform the digital mortgage application experience through innovative technology and customer-centric solutions.

Mission: Transform the digital mortgage application experience through innovative technology, thoughtful design, and customer-centric solutions that reduce friction and increase completion rates.
