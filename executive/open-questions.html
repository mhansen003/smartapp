Decision-Making Process

How to Get Decisions Made

1. Add New Question: Document in this page with impact assessment, recommended owner, suggested timeline. Include context and tradeoff analysis.
2. Escalate Blocking Items: Red items go to Paul Akinmade immediately. Cannot proceed without resolution. Weekly executive review of all blocking items.
3. Provide Options with Recommendation: Don't just ask questions. Provide 2-3 options with pros/cons and your recommended choice. Makes decisions easier.
4. Set Decision Deadline: Every question has target decision date. If missed, auto-escalate. Prevents decisions lingering indefinitely.
5. Document and Communicate: Once decided, update status, send summary email to stakeholders, update related documentation. Keep everyone aligned.
