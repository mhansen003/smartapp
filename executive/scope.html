Scope Change Management

How to Request Scope Changes

1. Document the request in open-questions.html with business justification
2. Estimate impact on timeline, resources, and dependencies
3. Present to stakeholders with tradeoff analysis (what gets deprioritized?)
4. Get approval from Paul Akinmade and project sponsor
5. Update documentation and communicate changes to team
