Objection Response
"Our current process works fine" "Fine isn't competitive anymore. Borrowers expect the Uber experience. Our competitors are modernizing - we need to lead, not follow."

"Timeline is too aggressive" "We're using proven tech and vendors. Bolt prototype validated the approach. P0/P1/P2 prioritization allows phased rollout. MVP in Q2, enhancements follow."
